Research interests

Combinatorics — extremal and probabilistic graph theory, Ramsey theory, and additive combinatorics — with a particular interest in computer-assisted verification of combinatorial bounds and in the interplay between finitary and structural methods.

I am also interested in formal verification and AI-assisted mathematics — in particular, in producing results that a machine can independently certify, an idea already present in my thesis and one I expect to matter more as AI reshapes how mathematics is done.
